 1357. A bill to extend authority relating to roving surveillance, access to business records, and individual terrorists as agents of foreign powers under the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 until July 31, 2015, and for other purposes; read the first time.  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the text of the bill be printed in the Record.  There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                                S. 1357       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. EXTENSIONS OF AUTHORITY UNDER THE FOREIGN                    INTELLIGENCE SURVEILLANCE ACT OF 1978.       (a) Roving Surveillance and Access to Business Records.--     Section 102(b)(1) of the USA PATRIOT Improvement and      Reauthorization Act of 2005 (50 U.S.C. 1805 note) is amended      by striking ``June 1, 2015'' and inserting ``July 31, 2015''.       (b) Individual Terrorists as Agents of Foreign Powers.--     Section 6001(b)(1) of the Intelligence Reform and Terrorism      Prevention Act of 2004 (50 U.S.C. 1801 note) is amended by      striking ``June 1, 2015'' and inserting ``July 31, 2015''.                                 ______                                       By
