 President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate proceed to executive session for theconsideration of Calendar Nos. 379 through 382 and all nominations on the Secretary's desk in the Coast Guard; that the nominations be confirmed en bloc and the motions to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table with no intervening action or debate; that no further motions be in order, that any statements related to the nominations be printed in the Record; and that the President be immediately notified of the Senate's action.
