Res. 1420. A resolution expressing the sense of the      House of Representatives regarding the terrorist attacks      launched against the United States on September 11, 2001; to      the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform, and in      addition to the Committees on Foreign Affairs, Armed      Services, Transportation and Infrastructure, Homeland      Security, the Judiciary, and Intelligence (Permanent Select),      for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in      each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within      the jurisdiction of the committee concerned.
