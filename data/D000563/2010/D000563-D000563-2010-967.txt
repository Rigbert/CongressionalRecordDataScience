President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 2 p.m. on Monday, March 15; that following the prayer and pledge, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed expired, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day, and the Senate proceed to a period of morning business until 3 p.m., with Senators permitted to speak therein for up to 10 minutes each. Finally, I ask that following morning business, the Senate resume consideration of the House message on H.R. 2847.
