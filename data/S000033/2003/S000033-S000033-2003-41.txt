 Chairman, I offer an amendment.  The Clerk read as follows:       Amendment offered by Mr. Sanders:       At the end of the bill (before the short title), insert the      following:       Sec. ____. None of the funds made available in this Act may      be used to make an application under section 501 of the      Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 (50 U.S.C.      1861) for an order requiring the production of library      circulation records, library patron lists, library Internet      records, bookseller sales records, or bookseller customer      lists.
