 President, for the information of all Senators, when the Senate convenes tomorrow, it will be in a period for morning business to be followed by the consideration of the House Labor-HHS appropriations bill as under the previous order. Amendments are expected to be offered and debated throughout the morning. Under a previous order, the amendments debated tonight with regard to foreign operations appropriations bill will be voted on tomorrow at 2 p.m. Any votes ordered relative to the Labor-HHS bill will be stacked to occur at the end of the series of votes in relation to the foreign operations appropriations bill. Therefore, Senators may expect votes into the evening.                          ____________________
