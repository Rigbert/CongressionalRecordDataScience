H.R. 184. A      bill to amend the Controlled Substances Import and Export Act      to provide authority to the Attorney General to authorize any      controlled substance that is in schedule I or II or is a      narcotic drug in schedule III or IV to be exported from the      United States to a country for subsequent export from that      country to another country, if certain conditions are met;      with amendment (Rept. 109-115 Pt. 2). Referred to the      Committee on the Whole House on the State of the Union.
