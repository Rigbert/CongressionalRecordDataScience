There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                                S. 2390       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON IMPOSITION OF DEATH SENTENCE.       (a) In General.--Notwithstanding any other provision of      law, no person may be sentenced to death or put to death on      or after the date of enactment of this Act for any violation      of Federal law.       (b) Persons Sentenced Before Date Of Enactment.--     Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any person      sentenced to death before the date of enactment of this Act      for any violation of Federal law shall be resentenced.                                 ______                                       By
