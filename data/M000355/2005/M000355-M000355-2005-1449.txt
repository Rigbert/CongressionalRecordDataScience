 President, I further ask unanimous consent that notwithstanding the provisions of rule XXII, at 11:45 a.m. on Tuesday, April 19, the Senate proceed to the cloture vote in relation to the Chambliss amendment, to be followed immediately by the cloture vote in relation to the Craig amendment. I further ask unanimous consent that at 4:30 p.m. on Tuesday, if the Senate is not proceeding postcloture, the Senate proceed to the cloture vote in relation to the Mikulski amendment, and upon disposition of the Mikulski amendment or a failed cloture vote, the Senate proceed to the vote on invoking cloture on the underlying bill; provided further, that in accordance with rule XXII, Senators have until 2 p.m. Monday to file first-degree amendments and until 11 a.m. Tuesday to file second-degree amendments to the Chambliss and Craig amendments.
