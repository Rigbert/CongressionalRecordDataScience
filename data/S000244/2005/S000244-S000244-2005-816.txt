House      Concurrent Resolution 216. Resolution expressing the sense of      the Congress that, as Congress observes the 40th anniversary      of the Voting Rights Act of 1965 and encourages all Americans      to do the same, it will advance the legacy of the Voting      Rights Act of 1965 by ensuring the continued effectiveness of      the Act to protect the voting rights of all Americans (Rept.      109-195). Referred to the House Calendar.
