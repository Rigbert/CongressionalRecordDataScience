President, I ask unanimous consent that the Subcommittee on Public Lands and Forests be authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on Tuesday, April 26, 2005 at 2:30 p.m., in room SD-366.  The purpose of the hearing is to review the preparedness of the Departments of Agriculture and the Interior for the 2005 Wildfire Season, including agencies' assessment of the risk of fires by region, the status of and contracting for aerial fire suppression assets, and other information needed to better understand the agencies' ability to deal with the upcoming fire season.
