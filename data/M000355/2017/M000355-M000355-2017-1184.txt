 President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m., Tuesday, June 13; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day, and morning business be closed; finally, that following leader remarks, Senator Paul or his designee be recognized, as under the previous order.
