S. 369       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. WRITTEN AGREEMENT RELATING TO EXCLUSION OF CERTAIN                    FARM RENTAL INCOME FROM NET EARNINGS FROM SELF-                   EMPLOYMENT.       (a) Internal Revenue Code.--Section 1402(a)(1)(A) of the      Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to net earnings from      self-employment) is amended by striking ``an arrangement''      and inserting ``a lease agreement''.       (b) Social Security Act.--Section 211(a)(1)(A) of the      Social Security Act is amended by striking ``an arrangement''      and inserting ``a lease agreement''.       (c) Effective Date.--The amendments made by this section      shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31,      2001.                                   ____                                       By
