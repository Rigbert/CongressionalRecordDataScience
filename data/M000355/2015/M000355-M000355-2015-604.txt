 President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 4:30 p.m., Monday, September 28; that following the prayer and pledge, the morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, and the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day; that following leader remarks, the Senate resume consideration of the message to accompany H.R. 719, with the time until the cloture vote equally divided between the two leaders or their designees.
