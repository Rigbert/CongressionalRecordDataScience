 President, for the information of all Senators, the Senate will begin consideration of another amendment to the campaign finance reform bill beginning at 9:30 a.m. tomorrow. A vote is expected to occur at approximately noon, prior to adjourning for the weekly party conferences. When the Senate reconvenes at 2:15, further amendments will be offered. By a previous agreement, there will be up to 3 hours of debate prior to a vote in relation to amendments. Therefore, Senators may expect votes approximately every 3 hours throughout the day.                          ____________________
