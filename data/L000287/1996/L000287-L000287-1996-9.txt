Mr. Speaker, the bipartisan ethics committee has said it anticipates completing its work by the end of this Congress. We owe it to the American people to dispense with this issue and remove the darkcloud hanging over this institution before the next Congress.  I urge all of my colleagues, Republicans and Democrats, just as the bipartisan Committee on Standards of Official Conduct did, to do the right thing. Let us return to the 105th Congress untainted by the charges of perjury, tax fraud, money laundering, lying to the Committee on Standards of Official Conduct.                             point of order
