(a) Findings.--Congress makes the following      findings:       (1) Ukraine is a major European nation as it has the second      largest territory and sixth largest population of all the      States of Europe.       (2) Ukraine has important geopolitical and economic roles      to play within Central and Eastern Europe.       (3) A strong, stable, and secure Ukraine serves the      interests of peace and stability in all of Europe, which are      important national security interests of the United States.
