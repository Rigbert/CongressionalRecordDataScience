 President, as in executive session, I ask unanimous consent that the injunction of secrecy be removed from the following treaty transmitted to the Senate on July 10, 2006, by the President: 2002 Amendments to the ITU Constitution and Convention, Treaty Document No. 109-11. I further ask unanimous consent that the treaty be considered as having been read the first time; that it be referred, with accompanying papers, tothe Committee on Foreign Relations and ordered to be printed; and that the President's message be printed in the Record.
