R. 4033. A bill to amend chapter 35 of title 44, United      States Code, popularly known as the Paperwork Reduction Act,      to ensure that Federal agencies give priority to reducing      paperwork burdens on small businesses having 50 or fewer      employees; to the Committee on Government Reform and      Oversight, and in addition to the Committee on Small      Business, for a period to be subsequently determined by the      Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as      fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned.
