Res. 71       Resolved, That (a) an individual with a disability (as      defined in section 3 of the Americans with Disabilities Act      of 1990 (42 U.S.C. 12102)) who has or is granted the      privilege of the Senate floor may bring those supporting      services (including service dogs, wheelchairs, and      interpreters) on the Senate floor the employing or      supervising office determines are necessary to assist the      disabled individual in discharging the official duties of his      or her position.       (b) The employing or supervising office of a disabled      individual shall administer the provisions of this      resolution.
