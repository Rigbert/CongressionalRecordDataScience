Mr. Chairman, I would like to remind my colleagues that H.R. 901 will not end participation by the United States in the Ramsar Convention. It does not do that. It has no effect on existing Ramsar sites, does not affect them at all. All it does is simply require any new Ramsar sites to be recognized and approved by its adoption.
