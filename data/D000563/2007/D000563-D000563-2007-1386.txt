President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it stand adjourned until 1 p.m. Monday, February 12; that on Monday, following the prayer and the pledge, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed expired, and the time for the two leaders reserved for their use later in the day; that there then be a period of morning business with Senators permitted to speak therein for up to 10 minutes each; that on Monday, Members have until 2:30 p.m. to file first-degree amendments and that the mandatory quorum required under rule XXII be waived.
