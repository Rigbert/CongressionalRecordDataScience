President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate proceed to executive session to consider Calendar No. 464, the nomination of Daniel Werfel to be Controller of the Office of Federal Financial Management, Office of Management and Budget; that the nomination be confirmed, the motion to reconsider be laid upon the table, no further motions be in order, and that any statements relating thereto be printed in the Record, the President be immediately notified of the Senate's action, and the Senate then resume legislative session.
