Speaker, due to my attendance at a meeting with President Barack Obama at the White House, I missed eight recorded votes on August 1, 2013. I submit how I would have voted had I been present for those votes.  On rollcall No. 428, the Waxman (CA) Amendment to H.R. 1582, had I been present, I would have voted ``no.''  On rollcall No. 429, the Connolly (VA) Amendment to H.R. 1582, had I been present, I would have voted ``no.''  On rollcall No. 430, the Murphy (PA) Amendment to H.R. 1582, had I been present, I would have voted ``aye.''  On rollcall No. 431, the Motion to Recommit for H.R. 1582, had I been present, I would have voted ``no.''  On rollcall No. 432, Final Passage of H.R. 1582, had I been present, I would have voted ``aye.''  On rollcall No. 433, Ordering the Previous Question on H. Res. 322, had I been present, I would have voted ``aye.''  On rollcall No. 434, Agreeing to H. Res. 322, had I been present, I would have voted ``aye.''  On rollcall No. 435, On Motion to Suspend the Rules and Pass H.R. 1897, had I been present, I would have voted ``aye.''                          ____________________
