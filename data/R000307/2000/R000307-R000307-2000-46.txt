President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate now proceed to the immediate consideration of S. Res. 304, which was reported by the Judiciary Committee.  The PRESIDING OFFICER (Mr. Fitzgerald). The clerk will report the resolution by title.  The legislative clerk read as follows:       A resolution (S.
