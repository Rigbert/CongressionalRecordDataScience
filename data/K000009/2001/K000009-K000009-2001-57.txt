Chairman, I ask unanimous consent that section 1031 that is a part of this amendment be replaced with the new version that was given to the desk and to both sides so that we could consider this in full.  The CHAIRMAN pro tempore. The Clerk will report the modification.  The Clerk read as follows:       Modification to amendment offered by Ms. Kaptur:         Strike section 1031 of the amendment and insert the      following:     SEC. 1031. FUNDING FOR CONSERVATION FUNDING.         (a) Reduction in Fixed Decoupled Payments and Counter-     Cyclical Payments.--Notwithstanding sections 104 and 105, the      Secretary of Agriculture (in this subtitle referred to as the      Secretary) shall reduce by $2,000,000,000 the total amount      otherwise required to be paid under such sections in fiscal      years 2002 through 2011, in accordance with this section.         (b) Maximum Total Payments by Type and Fiscal Year.--In      making the reductions required by subsection (a), the      Secretary shall ensure that--         (1) the total amount paid under section 104 does not      exceed--         (A) $5,123,000,000 in fiscal year 2002; or         (B) $5,224,000,000 in any of fiscal years 2003 through      2011; and         (2) the total amount paid under section 105 does not      exceed--         (A) $3,794,000,000 in fiscal year 2003;         (B) $5,317,000,000 in fiscal year 2004;         (C) $4,949,000,000 in fiscal year 2005;         (D) $4,785,000,000 in fiscal year 2006;         (E) $4,539,000,000 in fiscal year 2007;         (F) $4,058,000,000 in fiscal year 2008;         (G) $3,447,000,000 in fiscal year 2009;         (H) $2,885,000,000 in fiscal year 2010; or         (I) $2,495,000,000 in fiscal year 2011.
