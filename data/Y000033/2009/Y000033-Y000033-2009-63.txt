Madam Speaker, pursuant to the Republican leadership standards on earmarks, I am submitting the following information regarding earmarks I received as part of H.R. 3288, the Transportation, Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill.  Project Name: Port of Bristol Bay Expansion  Bill Number: H.R. 3288  Agency: Department of Housing and Urban Development  Legal name and address of entity receiving earmark: Bristol Bay Borough, 1 Main Street, Naknek, AK, 99633  Description of how the money will be spent and why the use of federal taxpayer funding is justified: Shovel ready dock expansion where the largest run of sockeye salmon is processed and shipped. This project is the economic engine for the low-income area of Bristol Bay. These funds will go toward constructing a sheet pile dock and addition to the 27 year old structure.  Appropriated Amount: $1,000,000  Detailed Finance Plan: Construction $1,000,000                          ____________________
