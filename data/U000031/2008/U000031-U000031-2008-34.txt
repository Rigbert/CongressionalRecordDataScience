Speaker, under rule XXII, clause 7(c), I hereby announce my intention to offer a motion to instruct on H.R. 2419.  The form of the motion is as follows:       Mr. Upton moves that the managers on the part of the House      at the conference on the disagreeing votes of the two Houses      on the Senate amendment to the bill H.R. 2419 (an Act to      provide for the continuation of agricultural programs through      fiscal year 2012) be instructed to recede to the provisions      proposed to be added to Section 9001 of the Farm Security and      Rural Investment Act of 2002 in the form of a definition of      ``Renewable Biomass.''                          ____________________
