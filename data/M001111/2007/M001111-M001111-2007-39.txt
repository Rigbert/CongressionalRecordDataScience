There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                                S. 2336       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. RICHARD B. ANDERSON FEDERAL BUILDING.       (a) Designation.--The Federal building located at 138 West      First Street, Port Angeles, Washington, shall be known and      designated as the ``Richard B. Anderson Federal Building''.       (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation,      document, paper, or other record of the United States to the      Federal building referred to in subsection (a) shall be      deemed to be a reference to the ``Richard B. Anderson Federal      Building''.                                 ______                                       By
