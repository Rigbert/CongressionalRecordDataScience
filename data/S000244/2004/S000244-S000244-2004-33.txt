Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 5363) to authorize salary adjustments for Justices and judges of the United States for fiscal year 2005.  The Clerk read as follows:                               H.R. 5363       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION OF SALARY ADJUSTMENTS FOR FEDERAL                    JUSTICES AND JUDGES.       Pursuant to section 140 of Public Law 97-92, Justices and      judges of the United States are authorized during fiscal year      2005 to receive a salary adjustment in accordance with      section 461 of title 28, United States Code.
