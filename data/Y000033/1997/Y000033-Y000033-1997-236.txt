H.R. 752. A      bill to amend the Endangered Species Act of 1973 to ensure      that persons that suffer or are threatened with injury      resulting from a violation of the act or a failure of the      Secretary to act in accordance with the act have standing to      commence a civil suit on their own behalf; with an amendment      (Rept. 105-42).
