Chairman, I rise in strong opposition to this omnibus bill that I believe is a major step backward for our Nation.  I am committed to insuring our Nation's fiscal integrity. Our obligation to our future and our children and to their children demands decisive and decidedly different action to affect a disciplined conduct of the fiscal business of this country.  But this Republican package is not the answer. It is an attack on the middle class and poor Americans.  I supported the balanced budget amendment to the Constitution. I voted for the Stenholm budget which would have achieved a surplus by the year 2002. I will also support the alternative reconciliation developed by Mr. Orton, Mr. Stenholm, Mr. Peterson, Mr. Sabo, and other Democrats.
