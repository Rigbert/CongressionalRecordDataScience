President, I ask unanimous consent that the Committee on Armed Services be authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on October 6, 2004, at 10 a.m., in open session to consider the following nominations: Francis J. Harvey to be Secretary of the Army; Richard Greco, Jr., to be Assistant Secretary of the Navy for Financial Management; and General Gregory S.
