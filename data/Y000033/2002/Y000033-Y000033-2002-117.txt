Mr. Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 4870) to make certain adjustments to the boundaries of the Mount Naomi Wilderness Area, and for other purposes, as amended.  The Clerk read as follows:                               H.R. 4870       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.       This Act may be cited as the ``Mount Naomi Wilderness      Boundary Adjustment Act''.     SEC. 2. BOUNDARY ADJUSTMENTS.       (a) Lands Removed.--The boundary of the Mount Naomi      Wilderness is adjusted to exclude the approximately 31 acres      of land depicted on the Map as ``Land Excluded''.       (b) Lands Added.--Subject to valid existing rights, the      boundary of the Mount Naomi Wilderness is adjusted to include      the approximately 31 acres of land depicted on the Map as      ``Land Added''. The Utah Wilderness Act of 1984 (Public Law      98-428) shall apply to the land added to the Mount Naomi      Wilderness pursuant to this subsection.     SEC. 3. MAP.       (a) Definition.--For the purpose of this Act, the term      ``Map'' shall mean the map entitled ``Mt. Naomi Wilderness      Boundary Adjustment'' and dated May 23, 2002.       (b) Map on File.--The Map shall be on file and available      for inspection in the office of the Chief of the Forest      Service, Department of Agriculture.       (c) Corrections.--The Secretary of Agriculture may make      technical corrections to the Map.
