 President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it stand adjourned until 9 a.m., Wednesday, October 24; that on Wednesday, following the prayer and pledge, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed expired, the time for the two leaders reserved for their use later in the day, and the Senate then resume executive session and consideration of the Southwick nomination, with the time until 11 a.m. equally divided and controlled between the two leaders or their designees, and the time from 10:40 a.m. to 11 a.m. divided and controlled between the two leaders, with the majority leader controlling the final 10 minutes; that the Senate then proceed to vote on the motion to invoke cloture on the nomination at 11 a.m., as provided for under a previous order.
