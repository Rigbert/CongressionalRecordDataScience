 President, I have a list of amendments on which I wish to propound a unanimous consent request.  I ask unanimous consent that following the disposition of the Sununu amendment, that Senator Lincoln be recognized to offer an amendment and speak for 2 minutes, after which the amendment will be withdrawn; further, that the Senate then proceed to votes in relation to the following amendments in sequence order; provided that there be 2 minutes equally divided between the votes and that no second-degree amendments be in order to the amendment prior to the vote: Schumer amendment No. 2635, Reid amendment No. 2653, Nelson amendment No. 2601, Bingaman amendment No. 2642, Durbin amendment No. 2623, and Nelson amendment No. 2625.
