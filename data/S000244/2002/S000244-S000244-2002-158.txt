Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the Senate joint resolution (S.J. Res. 13) conferring honorary citizenship of the United States on Paul Yves Roch Gilbert du Motier, also known as Marquis de Lafayette, as amended.  The Clerk read as follows:                              S.J. Res. 13       Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the      United States of America in Congress assembled, That Marie      Joseph Paul Yves Roche Gilbert du Motier, the Marquis de      Lafayette, is proclaimed posthumously to be an honorary      citizen of the United States of America.
