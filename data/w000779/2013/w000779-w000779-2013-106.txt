President, I would like to announce for the information of the Senate and the public that a hearing has been scheduled before Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources. The hearing will be held on Thursday, July 25, 2013, at 9:30 a.m. in room SD-366 of the Dirksen Senate Office Building.  The purpose of the hearing is to consider supplemental funding options to support the National Park Service's efforts to address deferred maintenance and operational needs.  Because of the limited time available for the hearing, witnesses may testify by invitation only.
