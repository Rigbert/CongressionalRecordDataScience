Speaker, I offer a privileged resolution and ask for its immediate consideration.  The Clerk read the resolution, as follows:                               H. Res. 4       Resolved, That the Clerk be instructed to inform the      President of the United States that the House of      Representatives has elected John A. Boehner, a Representative      from the State of Ohio as Speaker, and Karen L. Haas, a      citizen of the State of Maryland as Clerk, of the House of      Representatives of the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress.  The resolution was agreed to.  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.                          ____________________
