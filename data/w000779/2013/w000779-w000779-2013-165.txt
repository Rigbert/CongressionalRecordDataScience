President, I would like to announce for the information of the Senate and the public that a hearing has been scheduled before the Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources. The hearing will be held on Tuesday, May 7, 2013, at 9:30 a.m., in room 366 of the Dirksen Senate Office Building.  The purpose of this hearing is to consider S. 783, the Helium Stewardship Act of 2013.  Because of the limited time available for the hearing, witnesses may testify by invitation only. However, those wishing to submit written testimony for the hearing record may do so by sending it to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources, United States Senate, Washington, D.C. 20510-6150, or by email to danielle [email protected].senate.gov.  For further information, please contact Vickie Gunderson at (202) 224-5479 or Danielle Deraney at (202) 224-1219.                JOINT COMMITTEE OF CONGRESS ON PRINTING
