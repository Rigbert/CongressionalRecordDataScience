The Senator may recall that one of the first amendments on the floor--it may have been from Senator Bennet of Colorado, if I am not mistaken--said we are going to protect Medicare benefits, but we are going to try to cut the waste out of the current Medicare Program--the duplication and the overcharging that is going on--so seniors will not pay in terms of health care, but the taxpayers will not be held responsible for something that is not serving them well.
