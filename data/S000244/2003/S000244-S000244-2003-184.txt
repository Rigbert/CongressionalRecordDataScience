Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 2465) to extend for six months the period for which chapter 12 of title 11 of the United States Code is reenacted.  The Clerk read as follows:                               H.R. 2465       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.       This Act may be cited as the ``Family Farmer Bankruptcy      Relief Act of 2003''.     SEC. 2. SIX-MONTH EXTENSION OF PERIOD FOR WHICH CHAPTER 12 OF                    TITLE 11, UNITED STATES CODE, IS REENACTED.       (a) Amendments.--Section 149 of title I of division C of      Public Law 105-277 (11 U.S.C. 1201 note) is amended--       (1) by striking ``July 1, 2003'' each place it appears and      inserting ``January 1, 2004''; and       (2) in subsection (a)--       (A) by striking ``December 31, 2002'' and inserting ``June      30, 2003''; and       (B) by striking ``January 1, 2003'' and inserting ``July 1,      2003''.       (b) Effective Date.--The amendments made by subsection (a)      take effect on July 1, 2003.
