 President, I ask the Chair to lay before the Senate the House message accompanying S. 2012.  The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message from the House of Representatives:       Resolved, That the House insist upon its amendment to the      bill (S. 2012) entitled ``An Act to provide for the      modernization of the energy policy of the United States, and      for other purposes,'' and ask a conference with the Senate on      the disagreeing votes of the two Houses thereon.                            Compound Motion  Mr. McCONNELL. I move that the Senate disagree to the amendment of the House, agree to the request by the House for a conference, and the Presiding Officer appoint the following conferees: Senators Murkowski, Barrasso, Risch, Cornyn, Cantwell, Wyden, and Sanders.                             Cloture Motion  I send a cloture motion to the desk.
