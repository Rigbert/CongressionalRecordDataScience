 President, I ask unanimous consent that the Committee on the Judiciary be authorized to meet to conduct a hearing on ``Proposed United States--Chile and United States--Singapore Free Trade Agreements'' on Monday, July 14, 2003, at 4 p.m. in the Dirksen Senate Office Building Room 226.     Witness List  Panel I: Regina Vargo, Assistant United States Trade Representative for the Americas, Lead Negotiator for the Chile Free Trade Agreement; and Ralph Ives, Assistant United States Trade Representative for Southeast Asia, the Pacific and APEC, Lead Negotiator for the Singapore Free Trade Agreement.
