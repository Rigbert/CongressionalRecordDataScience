Mr. Chairman, I offer an amendment to the amendment.  The Clerk read as follows:       Amendment offered by Mr. Smith of New Jersey to Amendment      No. 9 offered by Mr. Roemer:       At the end of section 602(b), as proposed to be added by      the amendment, insert the following:       (4) Representation of family members of victims of      terrorist attacks.--Of the members appointed under paragraphs      (1) and (2) of subsection (a), at least one member appointed      under each such paragraph shall be a member of the family, or      a representative designated by such a family or families, of      an individual who died in the terrorist attacks against the      United States which occurred on September 11, 2001.
