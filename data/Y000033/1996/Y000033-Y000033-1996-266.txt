H.R. 1527. A      bill to amend the National Forest Ski Area Permit Act of 1986      to clarify the authorities and duties of the Secretary of      Agriculture in issuing ski area permits on National Forest      System lands and to withdraw lands within ski area permit      boundaries from the operation of the mining and mineral      leasing laws; with amendments (Rept. 104-516 Pt. 1).
