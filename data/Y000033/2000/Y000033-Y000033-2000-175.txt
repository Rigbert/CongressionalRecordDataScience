H.R. 4187. A      bill to assist in the establishment of an interpretive center      and museum in the vicinity of the Diamond Valley Lake in      southern California to ensure the protection and      interpretation of the paleontology discoveries made at the      lake and to develop a trail system for the lake for use by      pedestrians and nonmotorized vehicles (Rept. 106-923).      Referred to the Committee of the Whole House on the State of      the Union.
