 President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m., Tuesday, July 14; that following the prayer and pledge, the morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, and the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day; that following leader remarks, the Senate resume consideration of S. 1177; and finally, that the Senate recess from 12:30 p.m. until 2:15 p.m. to allow for the weekly caucus meetings and that the filing deadline for first-degree amendments be at 2:30 p.m.
