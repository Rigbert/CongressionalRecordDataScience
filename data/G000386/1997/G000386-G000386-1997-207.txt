 Res. 93       Whereas the family is the basic strength of any free and      orderly society;       Whereas it is appropriate to honor the family unit as      essential to the continued well-being of the United States;      and       Whereas it is fitting that official recognition be given to      the importance of family loyalties and ties: Now, therefore,      be it       Resolved, That the Senate designates the week beginning on      November 23, 1997 and the week beginning on November 24,      1997, as ``National Family Week''. The Senate requests the      President to issue a proclamation calling on the people of      the United States to observe each week with appropriate      ceremonies and activities.
