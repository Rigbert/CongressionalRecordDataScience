 President, I ask unanimous consent that on Monday, March 16, 2015, at 5 p.m., the Senate proceed to executive session to consider the following nominations: Calendar No. 17 and Calendar No. 18; that there be 30 minutes for debate equally divided in the usual form; that upon the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote without intervening action or debate on the nominations in the order listed; that following disposition of the nominations, the motions to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table with no intervening action or debate; that no further motions be in order; that any statements related to the nominations be printed in the Record, the President be immediately notified of the Senate's actions, and the Senate then resume legislative session.
