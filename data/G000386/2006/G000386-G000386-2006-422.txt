 President, I ask unanimous consent that the text of a bill I introduced today that may be cited as the ``America's Business Choice Act'' be printed in the Record.  There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                                S. 2345       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.       This Act may be cited as the ``America's Business Choice      Act''.     SEC. 2. EXCEPTION FROM DEPRECIATION LIMITATION FOR CERTAIN                    ALTERNATIVE AND ELECTRIC PASSENGER AUTOMOBILES.       (a) In General.--Paragraph (1) of section 280F(a) of the      Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to limitation) is      amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:       ``(D) Special rule for certain alternative motor vehicles      and qualified electric vehicles.--Subparagraph (A) shall not      apply to any motor vehicle for which a credit is allowable      under section 30 or 30B.''.       (b) Conforming Amendment.--Subparagraph (C) of section      280F(a)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by      striking clause (ii) and by redesignating clause (iii) as      clause (ii).       (c) Effective Date.--The amendments made by this section      shall apply to property placed in service after the date of      the enactment of this Act.                                 ______                                       By
