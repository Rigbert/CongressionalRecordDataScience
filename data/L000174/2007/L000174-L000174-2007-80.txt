President, I ask unanimous consent that the text of the joint resolution be printed in the Record.  There being no objection, the text of the joint resolution was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                              S.J. Res. 25       Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the      United States of America in Congress assembled, That, in      accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20      U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the      Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than Members of      Congress, occurring because of the expiration of the term of      Walter E. Massey of Georgia, is filled by the appointment of      John W. McCarter of Illinois, for a term of 6 years,      effective on the date of the enactment of this resolution.                          ____________________
