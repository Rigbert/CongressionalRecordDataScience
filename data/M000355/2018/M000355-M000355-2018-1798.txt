 President, I ask unanimous consent that following the resumption of the motion to proceed to H.R. 1865, on Tuesday, March 20, Senator Sanders, or his designee, be recognized to offer a motion to discharge S.J. Res. 54; further, that there be up to 4 hours of debate, equally divided between the opponents and the proponents of the resolution, and that following the use or yielding back of that time the Senate vote in relation to the motion to discharge.
