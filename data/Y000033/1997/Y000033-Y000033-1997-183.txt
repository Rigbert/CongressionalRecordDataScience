H.R. 858. A      bill to direct the Secretary of Agriculture to conduct a      pilot project on designated lands within Plumas, Lassen, and      Tahoe National Forests in the State of California to      demonstrate the effectiveness of the resource management      activities proposed by the Quincy Library Group and to amend      current land and resource management plans for these national      forests to consider the incorporation of these resource      management activities; with an amendment (Rept. 105-136, Pt.      1). Referred to the Committee of the Whole House on the State      of the Union.         [Submitted June 19 (Legislative day of June 18), 1997]
