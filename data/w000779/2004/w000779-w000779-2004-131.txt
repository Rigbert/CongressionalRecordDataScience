Res. 330. A resolution expressing the sense of the      Senate that the President should communicate to the members      of the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries      (``OPEC'') cartel and non-OPEC countries that participate in      the cartel of crude oil producing countries the position of      the United States in favor of increasing world crude oil      supplies so as to achieve stable crude oil prices; to the      Committee on Foreign Relations.                          ____________________
