 President, pursuant to the agreement of February 7 with respect to S.J. Res. 4, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate proceed to the resolution on Monday, March 26, at 2 p.m. and the time between 2 p.m. and 6 p.m. be equally divided between Senators Hollings and Hatch. I further ask unanimous consent that at 6 p.m. on Monday, the resolution be advanced to third reading and a vote occur on passage without any intervening action or debate, notwithstanding paragraph 4 of rule XII.  This is the Hollings constitutional amendment.
