This is a bipartisan amendment, supported by Senators Chafee, Coleman, Biden, Lieberman, Feingold, Reed, Lautenberg, and Landrieu.  Anyone who has read a newspaper or watched CNN over the past couple of months knows about the tremendous suffering in Liberia today.  Three-quarters of Liberians do not have access to safe drinking water.  Three-quarters are living in poverty.  Three-quarters do not have access to acceptable sanitation.  Eighty-five percent of Liberians are unemployed.
