H.R. 1070. A bill to amend the Federal Water      Pollution Control Act to authorize the Administrator of the      Environmental Protection Agency to make grants for      remediation of sediment contamination in areas of concern and      to authorize assistance for research and development of      innovative technologies for such purposes; with an amendment      (Rept. 107-587, Pt. 1). Referred to the Committee of the      Whole House on the State of the Union.
