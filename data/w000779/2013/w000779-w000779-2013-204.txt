President, I would like to announce for the information of the Senate and the public that a business meeting has been scheduled before the Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources. The business meeting will be held on Thursday, April 18, 2013, at 9:45 a.m., in room 366 of the Dirksen Senate Office Building.  The purpose of the business meeting is to consider the nomination of Dr. Ernest Moniz to be the Secretary of Energy.
