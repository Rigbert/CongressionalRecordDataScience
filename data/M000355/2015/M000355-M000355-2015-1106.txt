 President, I ask that the Chair lay before the Senate the House message accompanying H.R. 644.  The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message from the House of Representatives:       Resolved, That the House agree to the amendment of the      Senate to the title of the bill (H.R. 644) entitled ``An Act      to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently      extend and expand the charitable deduction for contributions      of food inventory,'' and further       Resolved, That the House agree to the amendment of the      Senate, with an amendment.
