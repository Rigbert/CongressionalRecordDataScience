Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 1914) to extend for 4 additional months the period for which chapter 12 of title 11 of the United States Code is reenacted.  The Clerk read as follows:                               H.R. 1914       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. AMENDMENTS.       Section 149 of title I of division C of Public Law 105-277,      as amended by Public Law 106-5, Public Law 106-70, and Public      Law 107-8, is amended--       (1) by striking ``June 1, 2001'' each place it appears and      inserting ``October 1, 2001'', and       (2) in subsection (a)--       (A) by striking ``June 30, 2000'' and inserting ``May 31,      2001'', and       (B) by striking ``July 1, 2000'' and inserting ``June 1,      2001''.     SEC. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.       The amendments made by section 1 shall take effect on June      1, 2001.
