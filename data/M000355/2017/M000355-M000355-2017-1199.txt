 President, I ask unanimous consent that following leader remarks on Tuesday, June 13, Senator Paul or his designee be recognized to make a motion to discharge S.J. Res. 42; that debate on the motion be equally divided between the proponents and opponents until 12:30 p.m.; that the Senate recess from 12:30 p.m. until 2:15 p.m. for the weekly conference meetings, and at 2:15 p.m. there be 10 minutes of debate remaining on the motion to discharge the resolution, equally divided between Senator Paul or his designee and the opponents; and that following the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote in relation to the motion to discharge.
