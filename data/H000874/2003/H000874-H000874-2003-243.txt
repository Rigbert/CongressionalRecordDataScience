Speaker, I will be glad to yield to the majority leader for the purposes of informing us of the proposed schedule for next week.  Mr. DeLAY. Mr. Speaker, I thank the gentleman from Maryland for yielding to me.  Mr. Speaker, the House will convene on Monday at 12:30 p.m. for morning hour and 2 p.m. for legislative business. We will consider several measures under suspension of the rules. A final list of those bills will be sent to Members' offices by the end of this week. Any votes called on these measures will be rolled until 6:30 p.m. Monday.  For Tuesday and the balance of the week, we will consider several additional measures under suspension of the rules. We will also consider the fiscal year 2004 Homeland Security appropriations bill; the Intelligence Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2004; H.R. 1, the Medicare Prescription Drug and Modernization Act; and the Fiscal Year 2004 Military Construction Appropriations bill; and, finally, we may consider H.R. 2351, the Health Savings Account Availability Act.  I thank the gentleman for yielding, and I am happy to answer any questions he may have.
