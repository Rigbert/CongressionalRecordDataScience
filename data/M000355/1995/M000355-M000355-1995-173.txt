 President, I ask unanimous consent that the Brown amendment No. 2708 be temporarily laid aside until 7 p.m. this evening, and at that time there will be 5 hours for debate to be equally divided in the usual form; and when the Senate resumes the amendment on Thursday, there be 1 hour remaining for debate to be equally divided in the usual form; and following the conclusion or yielding back of time, the Senate proceed to vote on the Brown amendment.
