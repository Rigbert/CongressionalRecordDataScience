Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 5472) to extend for 6 months the period for which chapter 12 of title 11 of the United States Code is reenacted.  The Clerk read as follows:                               H.R. 5472       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.       This Act may be cited as the ``Protection of Family Farmers      Act of 2002''.     SEC. 2. 6-MONTH EXTENSION OF PERIOD FOR WHICH CHAPTER 12 OF                    TITLE 11 OF THE UNITED STATES CODE IS                    REENACTED.       (a) Amendments.--Section 149 of title I of division C of      Public Law 105-277 is amended--       (1) by striking ``January 1, 2003'' each place it appears      and inserting ``July 1, 2003''; and       (2) in subsection (a)--       (A) by striking ``May 31, 2002'' and inserting ``December      31, 2002''; and       (B) by striking ``June 1, 2002'' and inserting ``January 1,      2003''.       (b) Effective Date.--The amendments made by subsection (a)      shall take effect on January 1, 2003.
