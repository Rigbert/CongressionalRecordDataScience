President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 11 a.m., Wednesday, March 11; that following the prayer and the pledge, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed expired, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use laterin the day, and the Senate proceed to a period of morning business until 11:30 a.m. with Senators permitted to speak for up to 10 minutes each with the time controlled by the Republicans; further, that following morning business the Senate proceed to executive session under the previous order.
