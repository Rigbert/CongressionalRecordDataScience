 With an $8.4 trillion national debt, a budget deficit that will exceed $300 billion this year, a looming entitlement crisis, and a mounting alternative minimum tax problem, full repeal of the estate tax at this time is simply not responsible.  We have until 2010 to make decisions about the estate tax. In doing so, time will afford us the opportunity to make more informed choices, with a more complete picture of our Nation's fiscal health.  We are talking about eliminating nearly $1 trillion in Federal revenues here, during a time of war.  Now is not the time to place the interests of a small number of millionaires ahead of millions of working families.
