  Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the text of the joint resolution be printed in the Record.  There being no objection, the text of the joint resolution was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                              S.J. Res. 29       Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the      United States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress      approves the renewal of the import restrictions contained in      section 3(a)(1) and section 3A(b)(1) and (c)(1) of the      Burmese Freedom and Democracy Act of 2003.
