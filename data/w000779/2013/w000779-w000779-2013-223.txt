President, I would like to announce for the information of the Senate and the public that a hearing has been scheduled before the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources.  The hearing will be held on Tuesday, April 16, 2013, at 10 a.m., in room SD-366 of the Dirksen Senate Office Building.  The purpose of this hearing is to consider the President's Proposed Budget for Fiscal Year 2014 for the Forest Service.  Because of the limited time available for the hearing, witnesses may testify by invitation only. However, those wishing to submit written testimony for the hearing record should send it to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources, United States Senate, 304 Dirksen Senate Office Building, Washington, DC 20510-6150, or by email to [email protected]  For further information, please contact please contact Meghan Conklin (202) 224-8046 or John Assini (202) 224-9313.
