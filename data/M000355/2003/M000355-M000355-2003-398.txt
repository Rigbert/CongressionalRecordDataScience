 President, I ask unanimous consent that at 10:30 a.m. on Tuesday, October 21, the Senate proceed to the conference report to accompany S. 3, the partial-birth abortion ban legislation, and that it be considered under the following terms: 4 hours for debate equally divided between the two leaders or their designees, provided that following the use or yielding back of time, the Senate proceed to a vote on the adoption of the conference report with no intervening action or debate.
