 Hatch, Ms. Stabenow, Mr. Reid, Mrs. Boxer, Mr. Kennedy, Mr. Cleland, Ms. Cantwell, Mr. Wyden, Mr. Thomas, Mr. Bingaman, Mr. Domenici, Mr. Jeffords, Mrs. Murray, Mr. Roberts, Mr. Feingold, Mr. Helms, Mr. Sarbanes, and Mr. Akaka) submitted the following resolution, which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary.                              S. Res. 255       Whereas the operation of correctional facilities represents      a crucial component of our criminal justice system;       Whereas correctional personnel play a vital role in      protecting the rights of the public to be safeguarded from      criminal activity;       Whereas correctional personnel are responsible for the      care, custody, and dignity of the human beings charged to      their care; and       Whereas correctional personnel work under demanding      circumstances and face danger in their daily work lives: Now,      therefore, be it       Resolved,      SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF NATIONAL CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS AND                    EMPLOYEES WEEK.       That the Senate--       (1) designates the week beginning May 5, 2002, as      ``National Correctional Officers and Employees Week''; and       (2) requests that the President issue a proclamation      calling upon the people of the United States to observe the      week with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
