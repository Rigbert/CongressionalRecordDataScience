A bill to add Kentucky State University to the list of schools eligible for assistance under part B of title III of the Higher Education Act of 1965; to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions.  I ask unanimous consent that the text of the bill be printed in the Record. KENTUCKY STATE UNIVERSITY QUALIFIED GRADUATE                    PROGRAM.
