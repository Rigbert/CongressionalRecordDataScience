 President, I further ask unanimous consent that at 9:30 a.m. on Friday, September 17, the Senate proceed to the consideration of the conference report to accompany H.R. 2587, the D.C. appropriations bill, and it be considered as follows: The report be considered as read, and there be 30 minutes of debate equally divided in the usual form.  I further ask consent that following that debate the Senate proceed to a vote on the adoption of the conference report with no intervening action or debate.
