H.R. 4586. A      bill to provide that making limited portions of audio or      video content of motion pictures imperceptible by or for the      owner or other lawful possessor of an authorized copy of that      motion picture for private home viewing, and the use of      technology therefor, is not an infringement of copyright or      of any right under the Trademark Act of 1946; with an      amendment (Rept. 108-670). Referred to the Committee of the      Whole House on the State of the Union.
