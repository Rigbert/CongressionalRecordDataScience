 Chairman, I ask unanimous consent that the amendment be modified in the form at the desk.  The CHAIRMAN pro tempore. The Clerk will report the modification.  The Clerk read as follows:       Modification to amendment offered by Mr. Sanders:       On line 3, strike 65,000,000 and insert 647,000,000.  The CHAIRMAN pro tempore. Is there objection to the modification offered by the gentleman from Vermont?  There was no objection.  The text of the amendment, as modified, is as follows:       At the end of the bill, before the short title, insert the      following:                 TITLE V--ADDITIONAL GENERAL PROVISIONS       Sec. 501. None of the funds made available by this Act      shall be used to maintain more than 647,000,000 barrels of      crude oil in the Strategic Petroleum Reserve.
