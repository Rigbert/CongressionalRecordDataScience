 President, I ask unanimous consent that on Tuesday, July 26, when the Senate resumes the Defense authorization bill, and notwithstanding the provisions of rule XXII, there then be 20 minutes of debate divided between Senators Collins and Lautenberg; provided further that following the use or yielding back of the time, the Senate proceed to a vote in relation to the Collins amendment No. 1377, to be modified to be a first-degree amendment, to be followed by a vote in relation to the Lautenberg amendment; provided further that no second degrees be in order to the above amendments prior to the vote.
