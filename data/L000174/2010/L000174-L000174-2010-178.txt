Sessions, Mr. Specter, Mr.         Schumer, and Mr. Lieberman):  S. 3518. A bill to amend title 28, United States Code, to prohibit recognition and enforcement of foreign defamation judgments in United States Courts where those judgments undermine the first amendment to the Constitution of the United States, and to provide a cause of action for declaratory judgment relief against a party who has brought a successful foreign defamation action whose judgment undermines the first amendment; to the Committee on the Judiciary.
