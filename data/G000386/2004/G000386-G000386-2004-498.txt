Witness List: Hon. Alan K. Simpson, Co-Chairman, Continuity of Government Commission, Former United States Senator [R-WY], Cody, WY and Prof. Sanford V. Levinson, W. St. John Garwood and W. St. John Garwood, Jr., Centennial Chair in Law and Professor of Government, University of Texas Law School, Austin, TX.
