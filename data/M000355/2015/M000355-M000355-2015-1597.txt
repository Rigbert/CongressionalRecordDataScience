 President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate proceed to executive session to consider the following nominations: Calendar No. 3 and Calendar No. 4; that the nominations be confirmed, the motions to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table with no intervening action or debate; that no further motions be in order; that any statements related to the nominations be printed in the Record, the President be immediately notified of the Senate'sactions, and the Senate then resume legislative session.
