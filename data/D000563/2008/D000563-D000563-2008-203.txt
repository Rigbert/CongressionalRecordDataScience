President, I ask unanimous consent that the conference report to accompany H.R. 4137 be set aside; and the Senate now proceed to the conference report to accompany H.R. 4040, the Consumer Product Safety Commission Act; there be debate on the conference report until 8 p.m. this evening, with the time equally divided and controlled in the usual form; that at 8 p.m. the Senate proceed to vote on adoption of the conference report to accompany H.R. 4040, that upon disposition of that report, the Senate then resume the conference report to accompany H.R. 4137 and the Senate proceed to vote on adoption of the report, without further intervening action or debate; that prior to the second vote, there be 2 minutes of debate equally divided and controlled in the usual form.
