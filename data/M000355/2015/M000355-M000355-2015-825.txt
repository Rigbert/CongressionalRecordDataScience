 President, I ask unanimous consent that at 5 p.m. on Tuesday, September 8, the Senate proceed to executive session to consider the following nomination: Calendar No. 82, Roseann Ketchmark to be U.S. District Judge; that there be 30 minutes for debate on the nomination equally divided in the usual form; that upon the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote without intervening action or debate on the nomination; that following disposition of the nomination, the motion to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table with no intervening action or debate; that no further motions be in order to the nomination; that any statements related to the nomination be printed in the Record; that the President be immediately notified of the Senate's action, and the Senate then resume legislative session.
