 Reading from the Elementary and Secondary Education Act, the Committee on Education in the Workforce, U.S. House of Representatives, page 54, under section 1120(c):       (c) Comparability of Services.--       (1) In general.--(A) Except as provided in paragraphs (4)      and (5), a local educational agency may receive funds under      this part only if State and local funds will be used in      schools served under this part to provide services that,      taken as a whole, are at least comparable to services in      schools that are not receiving funds under this part.
