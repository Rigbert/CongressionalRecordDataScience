The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message from the House of Representatives:       Resolved, That the House agree to the amendment of the      Senate to the bill (H.R. 1625) entitled ``An Act to amend the      State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 to include      severe forms of trafficking in persons within the definition      of transnational organized crime for purposes of the rewards      program of the Department of State, and for other      purposes.'', with an amendment.
