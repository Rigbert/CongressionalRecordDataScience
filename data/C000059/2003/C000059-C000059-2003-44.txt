Speaker, by direction of the Republican Conference, I call up a privileged resolution (H. Res. 34) election of majority membership on the Committee on House Administration, and ask for its immediate consideration.  The Clerk read the resolution, as follows:                               H. Res. 34       Resolved, That the following named Members be, and are      hereby, elected to the following standing committee of the      House of Representatives: Committee on House Administration:      Mr. Ehlers; Mr. Mica; Mr. Linder; Mr. Doolittle and Mr.      Reynolds.  The resolution was agreed to.  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.                          ____________________
