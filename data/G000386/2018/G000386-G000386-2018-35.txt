Committee on the Judiciary  The Committee on the Judiciary is authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on Wednesday, December 12, 2018, at 10 a.m., to conduct a hearing entitled ``China's Non-Traditional Espionage Against the United States.''                       Committee on the Judiciary  The Committee on the Judiciary is authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on Wednesday, December 12, 2018, at 10 a.m., to conduct a hearing entitled ``Oversight of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.''
