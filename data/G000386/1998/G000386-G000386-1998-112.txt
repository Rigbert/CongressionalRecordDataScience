 President, drug use among kids is growing dramatically. In the last few years, after a decade of decline, drug use is on the rise among 12- to 17-year-olds. The age for first use of illegal drugs has dropped. Today, the first-use of marijuana by 12- to 17-year-olds is the highest since we've been keeping records. The same is true for cocaine, heroin, and hallucinogens. We need to be talking seriously about how to stop this. This is why we ask our colleagues to support our resolution.  I send that resolution to the desk. I send it to the desk and ask that it be read for the first time.
