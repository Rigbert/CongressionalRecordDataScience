 President, I ask unanimous consent that notwithstanding rule XXII, at 2:15 p.m. today, the Senate proceed to the consideration of Calendar No. 178, the nomination of Christopher Wray to be Director of the FBI. I further ask that there be 4 hours of debate on the nomination, equally divided in the usual form; that following the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote on confirmation of the nomination with no intervening action or debate; that if confirmed, the President be immediately notified of the Senate's action. I further ask that following disposition of the Wray nomination, all postcloture time on the Newsom nomination be considered expired.
