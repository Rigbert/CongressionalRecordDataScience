House      Resolution 494. Resolution expressing the sense of the House      of Representatives that the United States has enjoyed the      loyalty of the United States citizens of Guam, and that the      United States recognizes the centennial anniversary of the      Spanish-American War as an opportune time for Congress to      reaffirm its commitment to increase self-government      consistent with self-determination for the people of Guam      (Rept. 105-751). Referred to the House Calendar.
