 President, I ask unanimous consent that the Subcommittee on Forests and Public Land Management of the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources be granted permission to meet during the session of the Senate on Tuesday, July 25, 1995, for purposes of conducting a Subcommittee hearing which is scheduled to begin at 9:30 a.m. The purpose of this hearing is to receive testimony on S. 45, Helium Reform and Deficit Reduction Act of 1995; S. 738, Helium Act of 1995; and S. 898, Helium Disposal Act of 1995.
