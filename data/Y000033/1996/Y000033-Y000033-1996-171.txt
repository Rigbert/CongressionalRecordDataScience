A bill to amend the Tongass Timber Reform Act to      ensure the proper stewardship of publicly owned assets in the      Tongass National Forest in the State of Alaska, a fair return      to the United States for public timber in the Tongass, and a      proper balance among multiple use interests in the Tongass to      enhance forest health, sustainable harvest, and the general      economic health and growth in southeast Alaska and the United      States; to the Committee on Agriculture, and in addition to      the Committee on Resources, for a period to be subsequently      determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of      such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the      committee concerned.
