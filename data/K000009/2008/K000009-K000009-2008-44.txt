Speaker, I rise this evening in order to consider the bereavement resolution in memory of our beloved colleague.  The Clerk read the resolution, as follows:                              H. Res. 1415       Resolved, That the House has heard with profound sorrow of      the death of the Honorable Stephanie Tubbs Jones, a      Representative from the State of Ohio.       Resolved, That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to      the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the      deceased.       Resolved, That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as      a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.
