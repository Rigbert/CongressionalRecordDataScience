 President, I ask the Chair to lay before the Senate the message to accompany H.R. 1295.  The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message from the House of Representatives:       Resolved, That the House agree to the amendment of the      Senate to the title of the bill (H.R. 1295) entitled ``An Act      to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to improve the      process for making determinations with respect to whether      organizations are exempt from taxation under section      501(c)(4) of such Code,'' and that the House agree to the      amendment of the Senate to the text of the aforementioned      bill, with an amendment.                Motion to Concur With Amendment No. 2065       (Purpose: In the nature of a substitute.)  Mr. McCONNELL. I move to concur in the House amendment to the Senate amendment to H.R. 1295 with an amendment.
