H.R. 1663. A      bill to clarify the intent of the Congress in Public Law 93-     632 to require the Secretary of Agriculture to continue to      provide for the maintenance of 18 concrete dams and weirs      that were located in the Emigrant Wilderness at the time the      wilderness area was designated as wilderness in that public      law; with an amendment (Rept. 105-192). Referred to the      Committee of the Whole House on the State of the Union.
