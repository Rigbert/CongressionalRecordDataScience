 President, I ask unanimous consent that notwithstanding rule XXII, at 5 p.m. on Monday, October 1, the Senate resume consideration of the House message to accompany H.R. 302, as if in legislative session; further, that at 5:30 p.m. on Monday, the Senate vote on the motion to invoke cloture on the motion to concur; further, that if cloture is invoked, the Senate remain in executive session and the postcloture time continue to run as otherwise under the rule; finally, that upon the use or yielding back of the postcloture time, the Senate vote, as if in legislative session, on the motion to concur.
