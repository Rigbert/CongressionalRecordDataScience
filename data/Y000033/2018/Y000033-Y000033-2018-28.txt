Mr. Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 6414) to amend title 23, United States Code, to extend the deadline for promulgation of regulations under the tribal transportation self-governance program.  The Clerk read the title of the bill.  The text of the bill is as follows:                               H.R. 6414       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF DEADLINE FOR PROMULGATION OF                    REGULATIONS UNDER TRIBAL TRANSPORTATION SELF-                   GOVERNANCE PROGRAM.       Section 207(n)(1) of title 23, United States Code, is      amended--       (1) in subparagraph (B) by striking ``21 months'' and      inserting ``42 months''; and       (2) in subparagraph (C) by striking ``30 months'' and      inserting ``48 months''.
