 President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate resume consideration of Senate Joint Resolution 18 at 11:30 a.m. on Tuesday, March 18, and that there be 1 hour remaining for closing remarks to be equally divided between myself and Senator Hollings; that the Senate then resume consideration of the resolution at 2:15 p.m. on Tuesday for 30 minutes equally divided; and, finally, following that time on Tuesday, the joint resolution be read for the third time and the Senate proceed to vote on passage of S.J. Res. 18 with no intervening action or debate with paragraph 4 of rule XII being waived.
