President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes it business today, it stand adjourned until 12 noon, Wednesday, October 31; that on Wednesday, following the prayer and pledge, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed expired, the time for the two leaders reserved for their use later in the day; that there then be a period of morning business for 60 minutes with Senators permitted to speak therein for up to 10 minutes each, and the time be equally divided and controlled with the majority controlling the first half and the Republicans controlling the final half; that following morning business, the Senate resume consideration of the motion to proceed to H.R. 3963, the children's health insurance legislation.
