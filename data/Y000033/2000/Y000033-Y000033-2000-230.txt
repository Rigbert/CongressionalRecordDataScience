H.R. 755. A      bill to amend the Organic Act of Guam to provide restitution      to the people of Guam who suffered atrocities such as      personal injury, forced labor, forced marches, internment,      and death during the occupation of Guam in World War II, and      for other purposes; with an amendment (Rept. 106-815).      Referred to the Committee of the Whole House on the State of      the Union.
