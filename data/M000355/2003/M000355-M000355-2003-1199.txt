 President, I ask unanimous consent that if the motion to adjourn is agreed to later today, the Senate stand in adjournment until 9:30 a.m. on Thursday, January 9; I further ask consent that on Thursday, immediately following the prayer, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed to have expired, the time for the two leaders be reserved, and there then be a period of morning business, with Members permitted to speak for up to 10 minutes each, until the hour of 11:30.
