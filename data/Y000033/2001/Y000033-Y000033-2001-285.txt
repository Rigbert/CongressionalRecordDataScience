Con. Res. 193. A concurrent resolution to express the      sense of the Congress that the Secretary of Commerce and the      Secretary of the Interior should direct the representatives      of their departments who are members of the United States      delegation to the International Whaling Commission to remain      diligent in their efforts to protect the ability of Native      people of the United States, who have been issued quotas by      the International Whaling Commission, to continue to legally      harvest whales, and for other purposes; to the Committee on      International Relations.                          ____________________
