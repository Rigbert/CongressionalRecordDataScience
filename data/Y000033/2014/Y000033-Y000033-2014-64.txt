Mr. Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 4120) to amend the National Law Enforcement Museum Act to extend the termination date.  The Clerk read the title of the bill.  The text of the bill is as follows:                               H.R. 4120       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. NATIONAL LAW ENFORCEMENT MUSEUM ACT TERMINATION                    DATE EXTENDED.       Section 4(f) of the National Law Enforcement Museum Act      (Public Law 106-492) is amended by striking ``13 years'' and      inserting ``16 years''.     SEC. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.       The provisions of this Act shall take effect as if this Act      were enacted on November 8, 2013.
