 President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 3 p.m., Monday, June 24; further, that following the prayer andpledge, the morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for use later in the day, morning business be closed, and the Senate resume consideration of the motion to proceed to S. 1790 under the previous order.
