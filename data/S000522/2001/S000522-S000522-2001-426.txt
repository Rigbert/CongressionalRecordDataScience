     H.R. 801. A bill to amend title 38, United States Code, to      improve programs of educational assistance, to expand      programs of transition assistance and outreach to departing      servicemembers, veterans, and dependents, to increase burial      benefits, to provide for family coverage under      Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance, and for other purposes;      with an amendment (Rept. 107-27). Referred to the Committee      of the Whole House on the State of the Union.
