President, the Committee on Veterans' Affairs would like to request unanimous consent to hold a joint hearing with the House Committee on Veterans' affairs to receive the legislative presentation of the Paralyzed Veterans of America, Jewish War Veterans, the Retired Officers Association, Association of the U.S. Army, Non-Commissioned Officers Association, the Military Order of the Purple Heart, and the Blinded Veterans Association. The hearing will be held on March 6, 1997, at 9:30 a.m., in room 345 of the Cannon House Office Building.
