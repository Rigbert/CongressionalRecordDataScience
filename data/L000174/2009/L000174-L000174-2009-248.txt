There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be printed in the Record, as follows:                                S. 1372       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. VEHICLE MAINTENANCE BUILDING.       The Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution is      authorized to plan, design, and construct a vehicle      maintenance building at its Vehicle Maintenance Branch in      Suitland, Maryland, to house, maintain, and repair      Smithsonian vehicles and transportation equipment.     SEC. 2. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.       There are authorized to be appropriated $4,000,000 for      fiscal year 2010 for the purposes described in section 1.                                  ______                                       By
