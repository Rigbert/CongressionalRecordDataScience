 President, I ask unanimous consent the Senate immediately proceed to executive session to consider the following nominations on the Executive Calendar, en bloc: Calendar Nos. 747 through 755, and all nominations placed on the Secretary's desk in the Air Force, the Army, the Marine Corps, and the Navy.
