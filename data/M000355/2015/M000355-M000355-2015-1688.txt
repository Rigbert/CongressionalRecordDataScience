The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message from the House of Representatives, as follows:       Resolved, That the House disagree to the amendment of the      Senate to the bill (H.R. 240) entitled ``An Act making      appropriations for the Department of Homeland Security for      the fiscal year ending September 30, 2015, and for other      purposes.'', and ask a conference with the Senate on the      disagreeing votes of the two Houses thereon.  Mr. McCONNELL. I move to insist upon the Senate amendment, agree to the request by the House for a conference, and authorize the Presiding Officer to appoint conferees.
