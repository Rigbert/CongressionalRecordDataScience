Joe Duffy back in 1994 in a letter to the gentleman from Florida [Mr. Diaz-Balart] stated, and I quote, ``TV Marti broadcasting is consistently being received by a sufficient Cuban audience to warrant its continuation.''  Havana, without question, is being heavily jammed. But outside of that area more people are able to pick it up. Plus areas near to Cuba--other islands and other countries--can also pick it up.
