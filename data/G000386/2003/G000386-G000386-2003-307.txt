  I ask unanimous consent that at 4:20 the Senate proceed to a vote in relation to Dayton amendment No. 957, to be followed by a vote in relation to the Lincoln amendment, No. 1002; to be followed by a vote in relation to the Lautenberg amendment, No. 982, with 2 minutes equally divided for debate for each succeeding vote after the first; further, that no amendments be in order to the amendments prior to the votes; and finally that the second andthird votes be limited to 10 minutes in length.  I ask unanimous consent that prior to the first vote, Senator Sununu be recognized for up to 5 minutes in order to offer an amendment.
