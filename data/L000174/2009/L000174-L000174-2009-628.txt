There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be placed in the Record, as follows:                                 S. 329       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF NONBUSINESS ENERGY PROPERTY CREDIT                    FOR PROPERTY PLACED IN SERVICE DURING 2008.       (a) In General.--Subsection (g) of section 25C of the      Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows:       ``(g) Termination.--This section shall not apply with      respect to any property placed in service after December 31,      2009.''.       (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by this section      shall apply to property placed in service after December 31,      2007.                          ____________________
