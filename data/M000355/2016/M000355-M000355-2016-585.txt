 President, I ask unanimous consent that at 5 p.m. on Monday, June 27, the Senate proceed to executive session for the consideration of Calendar No. 358; that there be 30 minutes for debate only on the nomination, equally divided in the usual form; that upon the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote on the nomination without intervening action or debate; that if confirmed, the motion to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table, the President be immediately notified of the Senate's action, and the Senate then resume legislative session without any intervening action or debate; further, I ask that at a time to be determined by the majority leader, in consultation with the Democratic leader, on Wednesday, July 6, the Senate proceed to executive session for the consideration of Calendar No. 357; that there be 30 minutes for debate only on the nomination, equally divided in the usual form; that upon the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote on the nomination without intervening action or debate; that if confirmed, the motion to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table, the President be immediately notified of the Senate's action, and the Senate then resume legislative session without any intervening action or debate.
