President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate completes its business today, it stand adjourned until 2 p.m., Monday, May 19; that following the prayer and the pledge, the Journal of the proceedings be approved to date, the morning hour be deemed to have expired, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day, and the Senate proceed to a period of morning business, with Senators permitted to speak therein for up to 10 minutes each.
