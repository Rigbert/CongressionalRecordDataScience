Mr. Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 1318) to name the Department of Veterans Affairs outpatient clinic in Sunnyside, Queens, New York, as the ``Thomas P. Noonan, Jr., Department of Veterans Affairs Outpatient Clinic.''  The Clerk read as follows:                               H.R. 1318       Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of      the United States of America in Congress assembled,     SECTION 1. NAME OF DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS OUTPATIENT                    CLINIC, QUEENS, NEW YORK.        The Department of Veterans Affairs outpatient clinic in      Sunnyside, Queens, New York, shall be known and designated as      the ``Thomas P. Noonan, Jr., Department of Veterans Affairs      Outpatient Clinic''. Any reference to such clinic in any law,      map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the      United States shall be considered to be a reference to the      ``Thomas P. Noonan, Jr., Department of Veterans Affairs      Outpatient Clinic''.
