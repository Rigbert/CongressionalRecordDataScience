 Chairman, I offer an amendment.  The Clerk read as follows:       Amendment offered by Mr. Sanders:       At the end of title V, insert after the last section      (preceding the short title) the following section:       Sec. 5  . None of the funds made available in this Act for      the Department of Health and Human Services may be used to      grant an exclusive or partially exclusive license pursuant to      chapter 18 of title 35, United States Code, except in      accordance with section 209 of such title (relating to the      availability to the public of an invention and its benefits      on reasonable terms).
