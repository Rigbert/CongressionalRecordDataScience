Bunn of Oregon):       H.R. 2278. A bill to prohibit the importation into the      United States of spent nuclear fuel unless licensed      facilities are in operation that have the capacity to store      or dispose of all nuclear spent fuel generated by commercial      nuclear reactors in the United States and from atomic energy      defense activities, and to allow local port authorities to      establish health and safety guidelines for safe shipment of      spent nuclear fuel; to the Committee on Commerce, and in      addition to the Committees on Economic and Educational      Opportunities, Transportation and      Infrastructure, and International Relations, for a period to be      subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for      consideration of such provisions as fall within the      jurisdiction of the committee concerned.
