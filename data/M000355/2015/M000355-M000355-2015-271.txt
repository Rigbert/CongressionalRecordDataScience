 President, I ask that the Chair lay before the Senate the House message accompanying H.R. 22.  The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message from the House of Representatives:       Resolved, That the House insist upon its amendment to the      amendment of the Senate to the text of the bill (H.R. 22)      entitled ``An Act to authorize funds for Federal-aid      highways, highway safety programs, and transit programs, and      for other purposes,'' and ask a conference with the Senate on      the disagreeing votes of the two Houses thereon.                            Compound Motion  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I move to disagree to the amendment of the House, agree to the request from the House for a conference, and authorize the Presiding Officer to appoint conferees.                             Cloture Motion  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I send a cloture motion to the desk.
