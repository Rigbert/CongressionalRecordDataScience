 President, on one final matter, over the past several weeks, the Senate has been working to bring much needed relief from the regulatory onslaught of the last 8 years. Using the Congressional Review Act, or CRA, we have already taken action to end regulations that threaten jobs, weaken our economy, and undermine States' authority. Today we will continue to move forward with our efforts to block more unnecessary regulations that hold our country back in a number of ways.
